Games

Football

Football is one of the most popular games in the world. It is a game of excitement and thrill with unpredictable turns.

Cricket

Cricket is a worldwide favorite involving two teams of 11 players, aiming to score the highest number of runs.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport with two teams of six players, each aiming to ground the ball on the opponent's court.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport with two competitors, typically in a ring, trying to outscore each other with punches.

Tennis

Tennis is played individually or in teams, where players hit a ball over a net using a racket.

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport with two teams aiming to shoot the ball through the opposing team's hoop.

Badminton

Badminton is played with rackets, where players hit a shuttlecock over a net aiming to land it in the opponent's court.

Swimming

Swimming is an individual or team racing sport requiring full body movement to navigate water.

Table Tennis

Table tennis is played with small paddles, where players hit a lightweight ball across a table with a net.

Golf

Golf is a sport where players use clubs to hit balls into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible.

Rugby

Rugby is a contact team sport where players carry, kick, or pass a ball to score points against the other team.

Hockey

Hockey is played on ice or field, where teams use sticks to hit a puck or ball into the opponent's goal.

Karate

Karate is a martial art that emphasizes striking techniques such as punching, kicking, knee strikes, and elbow strikes.

Skating

Skating involves gliding on ice or smooth surfaces using ice skates or roller skates, often for speed or tricks.

Archery

Archery is a skill of using a bow to shoot arrows at a target for accuracy and precision.

Fencing

Fencing is a combat sport with three main styles, where two opponents duel using foil, epee, or saber.

Squash

Squash is a fast-paced indoor racket sport where players hit a ball against a wall aiming for precision.

Cycling

Cycling is a sport or recreation that involves riding bicycles over various distances and terrains.

Surfing

Surfing is a water sport where participants ride waves on a board, aiming for balance and control.

Gymnastics

Gymnastics is a sport involving physical exercises that require balance, strength, flexibility, and coordination.

Skiing

Skiing is a winter sport where participants use skis to glide on snow, commonly performed on slopes or trails.
